Our Projects

Our projects showcase a diverse portfolio of interior design works, highlighting creative concepts tailored to residential, commercial, and hospitality spaces. Each project reflects our commitment to blending functionality with aesthetics, delivering timeless designs that align with our clients’ vision and lifestyle.

Tampines Street 42, Blk 456

This residential project was designed to create a modern yet welcoming home, featuring clean lines, warm neutral tones, and natural textures. The layout maximizes space and light, while custom furnishings and thoughtful detailing reflect the client’s personal style and daily needs.

Bedok North Avenue 3, Blk 328

This HDB apartment was transformed into a bright and functional living space, featuring an open-concept layout, sleek built-ins, and a neutral palette accented with warm wood finishes. The design balances practicality with elegance, tailored to the client’s modern lifestyle.

Jurong West Street 91, Blk 912

In this condominium project, we created a sophisticated yet cozy atmosphere by combining soft lighting, contemporary furnishings, and subtle textures. The design reflects the client’s desire for a serene retreat while maximizing comfort and style.
